About Jillian Halley

Senior Experience Consultant within Product Design at Slalom

Jillian is an accomplished experience design consultant with a wealth of expertise in crafting compelling digital products. Since 2016, she has been at the forefront of the industry, leveraging her keen understanding of user behavior and cutting-edge design principles to create intuitive and engaging user experiences.

With a strong foundation in UI/UX design, Jillian has collaborated with a diverse range of clients and industries. Her approach combines strategic thinking with a meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that every project delivers both aesthetic appeal and functional effectiveness.

Jillian is recognized for building alignment across product, engineering, and business teams while keeping accessibility and user trust central to every design decision.
